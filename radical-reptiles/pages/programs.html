Programs and Events

Daily Tours

Take a 45 minute guided tour with of our indoor reptile sanctuary. Meet our rescued snakes, lizards, frogs and more. Learn about animal habitats and take part in a feeding. Tours start every hour.

Questions?

Camps

Summer, Winter, and Spring break camps are available for campers age 5-12. Spend a week learning about and caring for our amazing reptiles. Contact us for more information.

Questions?

Birthday Parties

1.5 hour party packages available Saturdays and Sundays. Private animal meet and greet with pizza and cake to follow. Birthday child gets a custom t-shirt and a monthly pass. Contact us to reserve.
